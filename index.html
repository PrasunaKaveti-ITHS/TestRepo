Coconut Ladoo

About Recipe

This is a quick & instant 15-minute recipe of making coconut ladoo with fresh coconut, condensed milk (milkmaid) and cardamom powder. This is an easy recipe, gets done quickly and can be made even by a newbie in cooking. Generally, only the white part of the coconut is used.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh or desiccated coconut
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 2 pinch cardamom powder
  • 1 teaspoon clarified butter

Preparation method

  1. Heat 1 teaspoon clarified butter in a frying pan
  2. Add two cups of fresh or desiccated coconut in the pan
  3. Fry till it gives nice aroma
  4. Add one cup of condensed milk
  5. Cook until it forms dough
  6. Now add 2 pinch cardamom powder
  7. Let the mixture cool
  8. Make balls with this mixture
